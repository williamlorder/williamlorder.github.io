About

About Me#

My name is Zékai (Zachary) Lu. You can me Zachary for short. I am a PhD student in Sociology at McGill University (fast-tracking from MA program since 2024). My academic journey began with a Bachelor of Laws in Sociology from Guangzhou University (2019-2023), where I completed an awraded thesis. My Supervisor is Prof. Eran Shor.
