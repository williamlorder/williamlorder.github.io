About Me

Zekai Lu(陸澤凱) is a social science researcher from China who is currently a senior student in the Department of Sociology at Guangzhou University. His areas of interest include medical sociology, social psychology and computational sociology.Zekai hopes to focus on the field of medical sociology in his future PhD research.Zekai has already published many works on some English and Chinese journal.

Zekai’s favourite poem is by Du Fu,

窗含西岭千秋雪，门泊东吴万里船。

Research Interest

Sociology: Medical sociology; Computational sociology; Media Sociology and Gender sociology.

Psychology: Social Psychology(substance use disorder); Behavioral Psychology and Criminal Psychology.

Skill

Methodology: Traditional metrology(including Mixed linear models, Structural equation modelling, LCA/LPA and so on); Big data processing; Social network analysis; Machine learning(including Deep learning, and Natural Language Processing); Causal inference (Rubin Counterfactual Framework and Pearl’s DAG); Meta-analysis

Programming Language: Python(skilled), R(skilled), Stata(skilled), MATLAB, Netlogo, and Mplus(skilled).
