About Me

Lu Zekai is a social science researcher from China who is currently a junior student in the Department of Sociology at Guangzhou University.His areas of interest include medical sociology, media sociology and computational sociology.Zekai hopes to focus on the field of medical sociology in his future PhD research.Zekai has already published many works on some English and Chinese journal.

Research Interest

Sociology: Medical sociology; Computational sociology; Media Sociology and Gender sociology.

Psychology: Behavioral Psychology, Criminal Psychology and Social Psychology

Skill

Methodology: Traditional metrology(including Mixed linear models, Structural equation modelling, LCA/LPA and so on); Big data processing; Social network analysis; Machine learning(including Deep learning, and Natural Language Processing); Causal inference (Rubin Counterfactual Framework and Pearl’s DAG); Meta-analysis

Programming Language: Python(skilled), R(skilled), Stata(skilled), MATLAB, Netlogo, and Mplus(skilled).
